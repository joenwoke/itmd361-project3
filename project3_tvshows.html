My favorite TV shows

No 1: Family Guy

Family Guy is an animated sitcom created, produced and written by Seth McFarlane. The series follows the life of a family on Spooner street, Quahog in the State of Rhode Island. Peter Griffin(Dad), Lois Griffin(Mum), Meg, Chris, Stewie Griffin(Children) and their talking dog, Brian. The show follows the sick, twisted life of this family, with Peter being a "normal average Joe", Meg and Chris being the most unpopular kids in town, Stewie, the one year old baby that wants to kill his mother and their alcoholic self pretentious dog.

No 2: The Flash

The Flash is a series from the DC Universe. It follows the life of speedster Barry Allen who gets struck by lightning from the particle accelerator, giving him supernatural abilities to move roughly 8.3 million mph. This causes him to take on the life of a hero, using his superspeed to fight crime whilst discovering the truth about his past and future.

No 3: Friends

Friends is an American sitcom that focuses on the life of six friends living in New York. The characters of the show are Monica(Chef), Ross(paleontologist), Rachel(Fashion Designer), Phoebe(Masseuse), Chandler(data reconfiguration and statistical analyst) and Joey(Actor). The show follows their constant bickering and love for one another as they sort through life's struggles.

No 4: SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is an American animated series that takes place in Bikini bottom, a fictional city beneath the ocean where SpongeBob, a sponge, works as a fry cook for The Krusty Krabs, run by Mr Krabs, alongside his buddy Squidward, with frequent visits from Plankton trying to steal the Krabby patty secret formula. Not forgetting SpongeBob's best friend and star of his own show, Patrick.

No 5: Peaky Blinders

Peaky Blinders is a British drama about a notorious gang in the early 1900s. Set in Birmingham, England, Peaky Blinders follows the The Shelby Family, The Peaky Blinders and their leader, Thomas Shelby, a survivor of the Great War and a former Sergeant Major of the Army. The series is based on a true story of a gang named Peaky Blinders in the 1900s. It is believed that they got their name from being famous for hiding razor blades in their caps as they seek to earn power and move up in ranks in England
